Spaghetti Recipe

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Salt
  • Water

    • Instructions

    The instrutions for making spaghetti are given below:-

    1. Bring water to a boil.
    2. Add spaghetti to boiling water.
      1. Add salt to taste.
      2. Cook for 10 minutes, strring occasionally.
    3. Add marinara sauce to a pan, bringing to a simmer.
    4. Mix cooked spaghetti with marinara sauce.
    5. Enjoy.
